# About Voice of the Fathers

Imagine the church fathers were alive today. Would they spread the message of
Christ via podcasts or YouTube videos? Surely they would! 😉

This site presents writings from the fathers in easy to read typography, with no
ads or distractions. Each book or letter is also available in audio format, for
easy listening or download.

## Sources

Writings are sourced from a number of different places, with light edits for
this site. See each writing for its specific source material.

## Open source

The content of this site is entirely open source software. You can find it
[here](https://github.com/joshpeterson/voice-of-the-fathers)
