# Voice of the Fathers

This is the source code for
[voiceofthefathers.org](https://voiceofthefathers.org). It is a as Jekyll
website hosted via Github Pages.

## Development

See the [AGENTS.md](AGENTS.md) file for a full development instructions.

## Image generation

The image of each father is an AI-generated image, imaging the father in a
podcast studio, reading their writings. The current prompt is:

> Create an image of St. Ignatius of Antioch, depicted in the rich, formal
> attire of an Eastern Orthodox bishop, sits in a modern podcast studio
> surrounded by a microphone and sound equipment, wearing a pair of sleek, black
> headphones over his ears, his eyes cast downward, gazing at the manuscript in
> his hands, as if deep in contemplation, his face rendered in the vibrant,
> expressive colors and flat perspectives characteristic of traditional Eastern
> Orthodox iconography, with thick, heavy lines and gold accents, set against a
> warm, dark background that evokes a sense of reverence and intimacy, the
> overall mood one of quiet contemplation and spiritual reflection, the saint's
> skin tone a warm, golden brown, his features strong and dignified, with a
> prominent nose and a wise, compassionate expression, his eyes bright, dark
> pools of intensity, as if holding secrets and wisdom.

Each image is 512x512px in size.
